#Grown

Make your tasks more entertaining by growing a garden in the background. You get Coins every minute to place blocks in your garden 

##Controls: 
    Move: WASD or arrow-keys 
    Hotbar: Q/E
    Build-mode: 1 
    Delete-mode: 2 
    Use: Space 
    Rotate: R
